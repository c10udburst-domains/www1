Swift Programming Language

Swift is a general-purpose, multi-paradigm programming language developed by Apple. It is designed to work with Apple’s Cocoa and Cocoa Touch frameworks, and it is intended to be more resilient and easier to understand than Objective-C. Swift is known for its safety features, concise syntax, and performance. It is commonly used for developing iOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS applications. Additionally, Swift is open-source, which allows developers to contribute to its ongoing development and improvement.
