COBOL Programming Language

COBOL (Common Business-Oriented Language) is a high-level programming language that was designed primarily for business, finance, and administrative applications. It was developed in the late 1950s and has since become one of the oldest and most widely used programming languages in the world.

COBOL is known for its readability and simplicity, making it easy for programmers to write and understand. It is particularly well-suited for handling large amounts of data and performing complex calculations. The language supports a wide range of data types and provides extensive file handling capabilities.

In summary, COBOL is a programming language that is specifically designed for business applications, with a focus on readability and simplicity. It has been used extensively in the financial and administrative sectors for many years and continues to be in use today.
